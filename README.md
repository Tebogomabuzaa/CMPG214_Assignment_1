# CMPG214_Assignment_1: Designing Myself

## Identity Overview
My name is Tebogo "Junior" Mabuza. This project explores the transition from my heritage in Pretoria/Vaal to my professional rebrand in the Middle East, focusing on my upward path toward becoming a **Network Engineer** through the lens of a **Customer Service Representive**.

## The Visual Argument

  "Junior: Forged through global friction. I master internal networks beacuse growth and comfort never coexist. My Zulu grit and Dubai-honed EQ translate human struggles into resilient, high-scale hardware infrastructure."

## Symbolism
- **The Shield & Spear**: Represent my Zulu heritage and the grit required for technical resilience.
- **The Skyline**: Marks my global "initiation" in Dubai and the ambition to scale IT systems.
- **The Shout**: Symbolises my rebrand and the voice I found as an emphatic communicator.
